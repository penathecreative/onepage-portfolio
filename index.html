About Me

My journey began in Portugal and now finds me based in the Netherlands, where I orchestrate digital symphonies in code. With a bachelor’s degree in International Relations and four years of hands-on experience in customer support, I’ve led teams, solved complex challenges, and mastered the art of working with diverse talents.

From my early days as a gamer, my passion for technology was ignited, evolving into a lifelong pursuit of web development. But my story doesn’t stop there. I’m also a musician at heart. This duality fuels my creativity: I approach every project as if I were composing a new piece, blending technical precision with the rhythm and harmony of music.

Whether I’m designing intuitive user interfaces, building robust back-end systems, or exploring new frontiers in development, I strive to create digital experiences that not only function flawlessly but also resonate emotionally—just like a memorable melody.

I am always eager to collaborate on innovative projects, solve complex problems, and continuously grow as a developer. My goal is to craft seamless and engaging digital solutions that leave a lasting impact. Let’s build something remarkable together.

Meet

Full-stack Development

A serverless, progressive web application (PWA) built with React using test-driven development (TDD) methodology. The application integrates with the Google Calendar API to fetch and display upcoming events while offering advanced filtering capabilities, offline functionality, and data visualization. The project leverages AWS Lambda for backend operations, implements service workers for offline access, and employs comprehensive testing through Jest, Jest Cucumber, and Puppeteer. Key features include city-based event filtering, customizable event display options, offline accessibility, PWA installation capabilities, and interactive data visualization charts that categorize events by location.
